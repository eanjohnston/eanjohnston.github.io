Clear Space & Guidelines
Minimum Clear Space
Maintain clear space equal to the height of the 'A' in OAK on all sides of the logo.
Minimum Size
The logo should never appear smaller than 120px wide on screen or 1 inch in print.
Approved Backgrounds
White, Ivory Mist (#F2F4F3), Morning Mist (#E1EDE8), or any brand green. Avoid busy patterns or photography.
Don'ts
Never rotate, stretch, recolor outside the approved palette, add drop shadows, or place over low-contrast backgrounds.
